About Car Detailing

At Detailly, we believe your car deserves more than a quick wash—it deserves a full transformation. Auto detailing goes beyond basic cleaning. It’s a deep, restorative process that cleans, corrects, and protects every inch of your vehicle, inside and out.

✨ Exterior Detailing

We carefully wash, decontaminate, and protect your paint. Clay bar treatment, polishing, and waxing restore shine while guarding against UV rays and Kerala’s humid climate.

🪑 Interior Detailing

From carpets to dashboard, every surface is deep-cleaned and sanitized. Stains removed, leather conditioned, and upholstery refreshed for a spotless, hygienic cabin.

⚙️ Engine Bay Cleaning

A clean engine bay not only looks impressive but also prevents dust and grime buildup, helping maintain performance and value.
